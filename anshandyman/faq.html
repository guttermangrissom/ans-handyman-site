Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Most jobs start with a quick phone call or text. For larger or more detailed work, we’ll schedule a visit and provide a clear estimate.

How much does a handyman cost in Dallas / Greenville / Commerce?

Pricing depends on the job type, materials, and time required. We provide clear pricing and explain what’s included before starting work.

Common services include drywall repair, interior painting, gutter cleaning, fence repair, doors/hardware, ceiling fans and light fixtures, garbage disposal replacement, and general property maintenance.

Do you do rental turnovers and punch lists?

Yes. We work with landlords and property managers on punch lists, make-ready work, and ongoing maintenance.

Do you provide materials, or do I need to buy them?

We can supply materials for most repairs. If you already have materials, we can also install customer-provided items when appropriate.

Do you handle small jobs?

Yes. We handle small repairs as well as larger multi-item punch lists. If it’s on your list, reach out and we’ll tell you the best approach.

Are you licensed and insured?

We operate professionally and prioritize safety and quality. If you need proof of insurance for a property manager, ask and we’ll provide what’s required.

How soon can you schedule?

Scheduling depends on the current workload and job size. Call or text and we’ll give you the soonest available options.

Do you offer emergency service?

For urgent issues, call or text. If we can fit you in quickly, we will. If not, we’ll recommend the best next step.

What information helps you quote faster?

Send photos, a short description, the city (Dallas / Greenville / Commerce / etc.), and whether you want us to supply materials. The more detail, the faster we can quote.
